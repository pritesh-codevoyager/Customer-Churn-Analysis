# 🎯 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. Losing existing customers can significantly impact revenue and profitability.

This project performs an end-to-end Customer Churn Analysis using Python, Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib to identify patterns and factors influencing customer attrition.

The analysis helps businesses:

✅ Understand customer behavior

✅ Identify churn drivers

✅ Improve retention strategies

✅ Increase customer lifetime value

✅ Support data-driven decision-making

---

# 📈 Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to answer questions such as:

* Why are customers leaving?
* Which customer groups are more likely to churn?
* How do contract types affect retention?
* What is the relationship between monthly charges and churn?
* Which payment methods have the highest churn rates?

This project provides actionable insights to answer these questions through data analysis and visualization.

# 📊 Analysis Performed

## 1️⃣ Customer Churn Distribution

Analyzed the percentage of customers who stayed versus customers who left.

### Insights

* Overall churn rate
* Customer retention performance
* Business health indicators

---

## 2️⃣ Contract Type Analysis

Examined how contract duration affects churn behavior.

### Categories

* Month-to-Month
* One Year
* Two Year

### Business Insight

Long-term contracts generally result in higher customer retention.

---

## 3️⃣ Monthly Charges Analysis

Compared monthly billing amounts between churned and retained customers.

### Business Insight

Customers with higher monthly charges often exhibit a greater likelihood of churn.

---

## 4️⃣ Internet Service Analysis

Evaluated churn patterns across different internet service types.

### Categories

* DSL
* Fiber Optic
* No Internet Service

### Business Insight

Service quality and pricing can directly influence customer retention.

---

## 5️⃣ Payment Method Analysis

Identified which payment methods are associated with higher churn rates.

### Business Value

Supports optimization of billing and payment experiences.

---

## 6️⃣ Senior Citizen Analysis

Analyzed churn behavior among senior and non-senior customers.

### Business Value

Helps create targeted retention strategies.

---

## 7️⃣ Customer Segmentation

Customers were segmented based on Monthly Charges.

### Segments

💰 High Value Customers

💵 Medium Value Customers

💲 Low Value Customers

### Business Value

Enables personalized customer engagement strategies.
